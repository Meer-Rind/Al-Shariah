Al Shariah — Your Complete Islamic Companion

A beautifully crafted Islamic app designed to support your spiritual journey. From Quran recitation to accurate prayer times and Qibla direction, everything is organized in one peaceful place.

App Features

Al Shariah is built with a mission to make authentic Islamic content easily accessible to Muslims worldwide.

Quran Reading

Read the Quran Ayah-by-Ayah with beautiful typography and easy navigation.

Translations

Available in Urdu, English (Mufti Taqi Usmani & Jalandhari), French (Molana Hamid Khan).

Audio Recitation

Listen to the Quran in the beautiful voice of Qari Abdul Rehman Al Sudais.

Resume & Bookmarks

Continue where you left off and save your favorite verses with bookmarks.

Qibla Direction

Accurate compass-based guidance to find the Qibla direction anywhere.

Namaz Timings

Current and upcoming prayer times based on your location.

Islamic Date

View the current Islamic date and upcoming important dates.

99 Names of Allah

Learn and listen to the beautiful names of Allah with audio recitation.

Digital Tasbeeh

Keep count of your dhikr with this easy-to-use digital tasbeeh counter.

About Al Shariah

Al Shariah is developed with love and sincerity by a team of Muslim developers who aim to build a reliable Islamic digital resource.

We value simplicity, authenticity, and user privacy. Our team is based in Pakistan and we believe in creating peaceful digital spaces for the Ummah.

If you have feedback, suggestions, or Islamic content you'd like to share, feel free to contact us.

To provide authentic Islamic content in a simple, elegant, and accessible way to Muslims worldwide.
